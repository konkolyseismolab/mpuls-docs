Time integration
================

Using the method of lines (MOL) aprroach, the semi-difference flux formulas
convert the PDE system into a system of ordenary differential equations for
each grid points. Therefore the whole system for the $i,j,k$ gridpoint gives:

$$ \frac{\partial \boldsymbol q_{i,j,k}}{\partial t} = \boldsymbol f (\boldsymbol q_{i,j,k}, \boldsymbol r_{i,j,k}, t) $$
