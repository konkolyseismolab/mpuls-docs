struct CellCenter

Thermodynamic and local physical quantities defined at the center of each mass zone in the 1D stellar envelope model.

Following the idea of R. Smolec’s PhD thesis (2009), all intensive thermodynamic variables (T, ρ, p, κ, c_p, e_int) and turbulent quantities (E_q, S, D, D_r) are defined at zone centers. These represent the local state of the gas and are used in the static envelope construction as well as in time-dependent pulsation calculations.
